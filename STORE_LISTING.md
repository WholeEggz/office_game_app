## Subtitle (App Store, 30 characters) / short description (Play, 80 characters)

**App Store subtitle:** A hidden-role mystery at work

**Play short description:** A hidden-role mystery you play quietly at the office.

## Full description

> There's a mole in the building. Maybe it's you.
>
> Office Game turns your workplace into the setting for a slow-burn detective mystery — no meetings required, no one needs to know you're playing. A handful of your coworkers open a case together. Some of you are villagers, quietly going about your day. A few are something else, working together in the shadows, never fully sure how long they can stay hidden.
>
> Every round, the group votes. Every accusation costs something. No one is ever thrown out of the game — they're demoted, drawn back in, or turned into something new. The mystery unfolds over days, not minutes, in the background of a normal workday.
>
> This isn't a corporate game — it's an escape from one. Open a case with a few coworkers, and see how long the truth stays buried.
>
> **How it works**
> - Join a case with the coworkers already playing, or open a new one for your floor.
> - Every player gets a real, hidden role — most are in the clear, a few are not.
> - Cast your vote once a day. No pressure, no app notifications interrupting your work.
> - Uncover the hidden faction before they take over — or, if you're one of them, don't get caught.
> - No one's ever kicked out mid-game. The story keeps going for everyone, win or lose.
>
> Built for a small group to start — a dozen coworkers is plenty for a first case.
