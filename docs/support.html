Office Game — Support

Running into a bug, have a question about how a case works, or want to report something? Reach out:

contact@ultralearner.app

Before you write in

  • Include what you were doing when the issue happened (e.g. "joining a case," "casting a vote"), and what you expected vs. what happened instead.
  • If possible, mention your device and OS version (e.g. "iPhone 14, iOS 18.2").
  • A screenshot helps a lot if the issue is visual.

Common questions

I can't find the case I was invited to. Make sure you're registered with the same name you were invited under, and that the case hasn't already ended.

Is my role visible to other players? No — the app only reveals what the game's own rules allow at any given time. See the in-app "How to play" guide for details on what's hidden and when it becomes visible.

How do I delete my data? Email the address above with the name(s) you've used in the app, and we'll remove your records. See our Privacy Policy for more detail.
